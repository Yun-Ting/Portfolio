Problems

  • ♣ failed to differentiate target users of the home site and the developer site

    There are many overlapping information of the panlex home site (panlex.org)
    and the developer’s site (dev.panlex.org). We believe the target users
    of PanLex’s home site were meant to be people who explore this website
    for the first time.

    • ✘ Suggestion

    the home site should aim to provide basic information, such as a try it function,
    or even some visualization to assist novice users to grab the overall idea
    of this project. If the novice users want to dig further into the project,
    they can direct to the developer’s site for further information.

  • ♣ redundant links cause confusion

    there are many different links directing to the same page. For example, under
    the technology tab, there are 3 links in the list that direct users to the
    PanLex resource page, which is confusing and redundant.Besides, there are
    many reference links on the homepage, research page and try it page, which
    is not helpful in providing the overall idea of the project.

    • ✘ Suggestion

    unnecessary links can be transformed to plain text to reduce confusion.
    In addition, appropriate deployment of image would be helpful for first-time
    visitors to better understand panlex with very little effort

  • ♣ Inconsistency: unorganized internal and external links

    there are both internal links and external links placing adjacently in the same area,
    which confuses users in directing the website.

    • ✘ Suggestion

    a large amount of the external links direct the user’s from the home site to thedeveloper’s site
